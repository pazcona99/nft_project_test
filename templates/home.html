NFT Price Prediction Using Machine Learning

Cryptocurrency has become a household name and is an evolving technology that will only become more prevalent in digital finance. Using machine learning, the attributes of various NFT collections will be utilized to predict pricing and other analytics.

What affects the price of a NFT collectable?

  • The traits that increase price the most are the rarest: crystal glasses, body inverted, diamond dress outfit

  • The traits that increase price the least are the rarest: navy cap, yellow headphones, gladiator mask

  • The significance of a trait and its rarity do not always line up
